## Features

- **Dark Mode & Modern Design** — Professional dark theme with green accent
- **Single-Page Scroll Layout** — Smooth scroll with active section highlighting
- **Responsive Design** — Desktop, Tablet, Mobile with burger menu
- **Profile Photo Loop** — 6 photos cycling with crossfade animation
- **Typing Effect** — Animated role titles in the hero section
- **Loading Screen** — Branded NK loader on page entry
- **Scroll Progress Bar** — Green progress indicator at page top
- **Skills Section** — 7 tabbed categories with official tool logos
- **Projects Section** — 9 professional + 5 personal projects with filters
- **Blogs Section** — 5 published technical articles with direct links
- **Certifications** — Thumbnail cards with full-screen lightbox on click
- **Contact Form** — EmailJS-powered form sending directly to Gmail
- **Download CV** — One-click resume PDF download
- **Back to Top** — Smooth scroll button
- **AOS Animations** — Scroll-triggered reveal animations throughout
